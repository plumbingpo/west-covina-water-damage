Who We Are

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

West Covina Restoration was founded with a simple mission: provide West Covina homeowners and businesses with restoration services they can trust. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a full-service team of IICRC-certified technicians, but our dedication to quality has remained the same.
